Rules

Rules to the game:

  • First you must login
  • Pick your team
    • One player: ranked 1-10
    • Two players: ranked 11-30
    • Two players: ranked 31+
  • Must submit team midnight before tournament begins
  • Payment submitted before tournament starts:
    • Your team will be removed otherwise
  • Winner takes all - 10% for commissioner
  • Tiebreaker:
    • Multiple teams with the same score
    • Add whatever place your players finished
    • Example: player#1: 3rd place, player#2: 4th place, player#3:
      5th place, player#4: 10th place
    • Your score is 22 (3+4+5+10)
    • Lowest scores wins tie breaker
    • If teams still have same score you split
